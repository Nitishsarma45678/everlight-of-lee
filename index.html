For July 4th

My dearest Lilly,

If there really were enchanted forests, ancient spells, and kingdoms beyond this world, none of their magic could mean to me what you do.

You are the sweetest and kindest soul I have ever known. You feel everything deeply, love people completely, speak your heart honestly, and somehow still choose to love me through all of me.

For three years, you have lifted weights you did not place on my shoulders. When life becomes cruel and I am lost inside it, you are the person I run to. You do not merely make my life happier. You make it feel like it has somewhere to return to.

So on your birthday, I want you to remember this: you deserve to be treated like a queen, not only today, but in every ordinary little day that follows. Even on the short-tempered days. Especially on the lazy ones.

In every world, in every story, I would still look for you.
And when I found you, I would know I was home.

Happy Birthday, my Lee. All my love, always.
Nitish
