# The Everlight of Leela

A self-contained interactive birthday fairytale made for Leela ("Lilly" / "Lee").

## Open it

Double-click `index.html`. No installation or internet connection is required.

## Experience

- Seven illustrated story scenes
- Interactive rune, magical qualities, and three lanterns
- Generated ambient forest sound and chimes
- July 4 birthday countdown
- Responsive phone and desktop layouts
- No photos, frameworks, external fonts, or hosted assets

The final wording and story are in `index.html`. Visual styling is in
`styles.css`, and interactions are in `script.js`.
